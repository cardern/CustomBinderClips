What are Custom Clips?

No Matter the Industry

Professionals from a variety of different industries have all been able to use Custom Clips in unique ways.

Banking- When a bank changed its name it provided all of its drive-through customers with Custom Clips displaying its new logo.

Accounting- A firm decided to capitalize on opportunities to build its brand name by including Custom Clips with tax returns sent to clients.

Printing- A full service printing company uses Custom Clips to market the organization to potential customers. When it sends a brochure, a Custom Clip is included.


Insurance- A title insurance company has Custom Clips to accompany all real estate closing documents. The clips have been very effective in keeping the company name visible with real estate brokers and attorneys and help the company to compete with larger insurance providers.

Medicine- By attaching a Custom Clip to hold original and duplicate X-Rays together, an X-Ray duplication company was able to change a simple X-Ray into an effective tool for marketing its services.

Law Firm- A firm wanted to distinguish itself from other firms by using Custom Clips when groups of documents were sent.



Promotional Advertising

Custom Clips are effective for marketing because people will keep them for a long time. They are great giveaway items for trade shows- they are unique and practical, and people really use them. Four color logos look great on Custom Clips and all three sides of the clip provide highly visible advertising space.

Artwork Specifications

Format- Please set up your artwork according to the template linked here. Include 1/8" bleed if necessary. Files should be submitted in .jpeg or .tif format at 300 dpi to size. For the medium clip, sides 1 & 2 should be 0.5625", top should be 0.625", and width should be 1.25". For the large clip, sides 1 & 2 should be 1.03125", top should be 1.0", and width should be 1.9375".

Colors- Full color labels are provided at no additional charge. Please note, labels are printed in 4-color process. PMS color matching is available for orders over 20,000 units and will require extra lead time.

Proofs- A color proof will be emailed to you for approval before we begin production. Production time does not begin until proof is approved. Color proofs will not match exactly but they will be a good representation of printing. Proofs are provided at no additional charge.
